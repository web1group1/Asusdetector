TROUBLESHOOTING MODE

ROG 230W DC Adapter

Please identify your problem

Damaged cable

run your finger through the cable and if you feel a break in the cable then it has most likely snapped

if its the IEC C14 cable that snapped dont worry it is easily replacable

If it is the undetatchable cable on the asus charger thats broken then you need to buy a replacement charger

Overheating

if the charger block is overheating there might be a few reasons why it could be happening

it could be because of the outlets being dirty, try cleaning them with a vaccuum

age could play a factor in it, if its old then you should get it replaced as it might be danagerous

if the charger is fully broken you can awlays buy a new one

factory defect, if it is new and still experiencing overheating it might be due to a flaw in the production process in which you should immediately contact asus support
